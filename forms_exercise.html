Banana Chocolate Chip Muffin Recipe

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup room temp butter
  • 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 very ripe bananas, mashed well
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • Coarse sanding sugar, for the tops

Description

These muffins - soft at the bottom and crunchy at the top. Melted chocolate chips and warm banana slices inside. Delightful for any time of day.

Directions

  1. Heat the oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Cream the butter and sugar until light. Add the egg, beating well. Stir in the vanilla.
  3. Sift together the flour, salt and baking powder, and add to the creamed mixture alternately with the mashed banana: flour, banana, flour, banana, ending on flour. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  4. Line a standard muffin tin with 6 liners, and fill equally with the batter. (Give or take 1 muffin: the exact number of muffins you will end up with depends on the size of your banana)
  5. Sprinkle the sanding sugar over the tops of the muffins, and bake at for 30-35 minutes, or until the tops are golden and toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Remove muffins from tin, cool, and enjoy!
